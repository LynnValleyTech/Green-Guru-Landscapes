About Green Guru Landscapes

Founded in 2010, Green Guru Landscapes has been beautifying North Vancouver properties with sustainable, innovative landscaping solutions.

Our team of certified horticulturists and landscape designers brings together creativity, technical expertise, and a deep respect for the natural environment.

We're proud to be a leader in eco-friendly landscaping practices, using native plants, organic methods, and water-wise techniques to create outdoor spaces that are as sustainable as they are beautiful.
